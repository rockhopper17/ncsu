\section{Budget}
The objective of the STARS mission is to characterize the inner Van Allen radiation Belt. This is important because radiation inside of belts like these can affect potential operations, either within the belts themselves or in transit through the belt. Understanding the inner Van Allen Belt, as well as the others, will help future space projects that intend to go near or through them.

While the budget for this project is theoretical, there is still a limit. Therefore, it has to be made sure that the budget is maintained and not exceeded. The first step is to receive a desired parts list from each of the subsystems. This will occur after each subsystem has chosen which part is the best through their analysis. With this list of parts, comparisons will be done on different Cubesat part websites. This comparison will be between prices as well as quality because sometimes the cheapest option isn't the best. While this is being done, a cost model will be created. This needs to be created because the price of the parts will change over time and that change needs to be tracked since this is a multi-year project.
